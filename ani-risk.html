Scoring Algorithm

How ANI Risk is Calculated

The ANI Risk Score (0-100) is calculated dynamically by evaluating daily historical dialing patterns. Points are assigned based on negative performance indicators, fatigue velocity, and carrier block patterns.

Risk Factors (Points)

  • Volume: > 1k calls +30 pts
  • Volume: > 500 calls +15 pts
  • Velocity: > 100 Dials/Day +20 pts
  • Velocity: > 50 Dials/Day +10 pts
  • Ans. Machine: > 60% +25 pts
  • Ans. Machine: > 40% +15 pts
  • Contact Rate: < 15% +20 pts
  • Contact Rate: < 25% +10 pts
  • Bad Numbers: > 10% +25 pts

Status Thresholds

Burned (Quarantine)

Score ≥ 75. High probability of being flagged as Scam Likely or blocked by carriers.

At Risk (Rotate)

Score 45 - 74. Showing signs of fatigue, recommended for resting or rotation.

Clean (Keep Active)

Score < 45. Healthy KPIs, safe to continue dialing.

Monitor

Under 50 total calls. Insufficient historical data to accurately assign a risk score.
