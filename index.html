Data Privacy & Processing Architecture

Dear Dially is built on a zero-trust, absolute-privacy framework. It operates exclusively within the confines of your local machine's browser memory.

  • Zero Uploads: Your CSV files never leave your computer. There is no backend server or database receiving your data.
  • Local Hardware: All parsing, matrix calculations, and rendering occur using your own device's CPU.
  • Ephemeral State: Once you close or refresh this tab, all data arrays are permanently erased from existence.

Data Sources & Calculations

  • Total Calls: Sum of Volume count across raw CSV structure.
  • Outbound: Calls parsed where "CALL TYPE" explicitly includes OUTBOUND string.
  • Manual / Preview: Explicit metric match against string logic containing MANUAL or PREVIEW.
  • Contacted Count: Aggregation of rows where TALK TIME > 0 OR ABANDONED = 1.
  • Connect & Success Count: Derived exclusively from Disposition Settings configured manually by user.
  • Contact Rate: Contacted / Total Calls.
  • Connect Rate: Connects / Total Calls.
  • Success Rate: Successes / Connects.
  • Abandon Rate: Abandoned / Contacted.
